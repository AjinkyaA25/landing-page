Experiences

Software Engineering Intern – Home Depot

During my time as a Software Engineering Intern at Home Depot (May 2025 – Present), I worked on initiatives to improve pricing accuracy for Pro customers. I built a notification service using the Spring Boot framework that helped pricing managers identify mismatches, while also learning how large-scale systems handle millions of sales records through SQL and Google BigQuery. I also explored how predictive sales models can be applied to real-world pricing adjustments, gaining experience in both backend development and data-driven decision making. This role taught me the importance of balancing technical problem solving with business impact, and how software solutions can directly affect customers at scale.

Digital Creator – YouTube

Creating more than 20 educational videos taught me how to break down complex mechanics into digestible content for a global audience of over 2,000 viewers across 60+ countries. In the process, I strengthened my skills in video editing, marketing, and storytelling, while also learning how to engage a community and track growth using analytics.
